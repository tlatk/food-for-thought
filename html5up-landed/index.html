About Us

Opening Hours

  • Mon to Fri - 8AM to 8PM
  • Sat & Sun - 10AM to 8PM

We are a non-profit based restaurant who aims to tackle the issue of food waste in super markets. We work with local super markets and distributors to make changable daily menus using diverted fresh food that would otherwise be sent to landfill.

We deliver a pay as you feel service which caters to students, financial unstable families and people passionate about the issue of food waste. If you can't afford to pay money voluntary work is also a form of payment.

About Food Waste

Despite what the name suggests food waste does not refer to rotten food but fresh food that is thrown out due to being near it's due date. Food waste is a serious issue that exists in society, listed below are some shocking facts about food waste.

  • Over 1/3 of all food gloablly produced goes to waste
  • Annual value of global food waste is $1 trillion
  • The world's nearly 1 billion hungry people could be fed with the food waste in the US and EU.
